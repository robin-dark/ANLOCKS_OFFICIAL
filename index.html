About Rahul Bakers

Serving Mathura Since 2012

Welcome to Rahul Bakers, a renowned name in Mathura for quality items and delicious fast food. Located in the heart of Krishna Nagar and Dampier Nagar, we have been serving our community with love for over a decade.

Whether you are looking for a custom birthday cake, a quick patty snack, or a family dinner with Chinese delicacies, we are your go-to destination. Our commitment is simple: Quality ingredients, great taste, and a smile on your face.
